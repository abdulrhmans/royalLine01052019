Invoice Source Document Button

This module adds a 'Source Document' button to your Vendor bills and Invoices where you can have a direct link & access to their related Purhase Orders & Sale Orders.

User Guide

Navigate to the Sales or Purchase menu in Accounting and select any Invoice or Vendor bill.

Click on the 'Source Document' button and you will be taken to the Sale Order or Purchase Order which this Invoice originates from.
