User Guide

Open your Users & Companies menu, navigate to Login Trials.

Attempt status is 'Success', the password does not appear. When the status is 'Fail' however, Only Asterisks are shown to protect privacy.

If a user types their password wrong 4 times, they are automatically blocked until either manually removed by someone with access, or until a cronjob that works every 10 minutes automatically removes them (configurable).

Restricting Location:

Open your Users & Companies menu, navigate to Location.

Create a location with an Employee or Company Branch's IP address.

Restrict the Employee or Company Branch to only entering from their IP address.

Open your Users & Companies menu, navigate to Login Trials. Since another Employee (B)'s IP address was already defined, the location from which Employee A tried to log on showed.
