User Guide

Main Configurations:

1-Setting the accounting policy.

Set the accounting policy using two ways as seen below :
A-One Account For All Employees
B-Account Per Employee
WARNING: Changing between the two policies (while loan records in place) may cause unwanted results, so be advised to study and choose the most suitable policy from day one.

Note: the accounts in the previous two points have to be reconcilable accounts.
Note:each employee will have a related user to avoid extra charges for users we recommend to deactivate the employees related users.

2-Add the loan salary rule to the salary structure(s) of your company where applicable.

Max Loan Percentage (%): indicates the allowed percentage of the employee wage to be requested as a loan (take in consideration the previous loans balance for the same employee).
Interest Rate: Indicates the interest rate to be added to the loan amount.

3-Define the loan types.

Loan Creation

To create a new loan go to Payroll-loans-create.

...

At 'Approved stage 'payment' smart button will appear.

'Payment' is the payment of loan, it will appear in the 'Accounting' Module as a vendor payment.
At the creation of the employee payslip the loan will appear under 'Other Input' section.
After sheet computation 'Loans Rule' will appear under the 'Salary Computation' tab.

After the confirmation of the payslip the loan amount allocated to the payslip month will be deducted from the loan balance.

At 'Approved' stage 'Receipt Voucher' button will appear.
'Receipt Voucher' is responsible for the payments the employee pays in advance (non-payslip payments).

Once you create a receipt voucher it will be listed under 'Receipts' smart button.
The reflection of the receipt voucher is in 'Accounting' module as a customer payment.
